Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how WordHaven ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

WordHaven does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require account registration, do not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information, and do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

All game progress (levels completed, coin balance, puzzle history) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by WordHaven. You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites .

On iOS, you will be asked for permission to track (ATT prompt) before any personalized advertising data is collected. You may opt out at any time in your device Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.
