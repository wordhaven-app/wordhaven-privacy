Privacy Policy

WordHaven  ·  Effective date: March 2026

WordHaven is a word puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use our app and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

WordHaven does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All game data is stored locally on your device only.

  • Game progress — your current level, discovered words, coin balance, and settings are saved in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No account required — we do not ask you to create an account or sign in.
  • No analytics — we do not use third-party analytics services.
  • No advertising — WordHaven contains no advertisements.
  • No tracking — we do not track you across apps or websites.

2. Data Storage

Game data is stored locally on your device using standard browser/WebView storage (localStorage). This data is not backed up to any server and is not accessible to us. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

3. Children's Privacy

WordHaven does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because we collect no personal information from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

4. Third-Party Services

WordHaven does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. No data is shared with any third party.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy in a future version of the app, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@wordhaven.app
