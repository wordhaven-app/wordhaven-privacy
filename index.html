Plain-language summary: WordHaven does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Your game progress lives only on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how WordHaven ("we", "our", "the app", or "the developer") handles information in connection with your use of the WordHaven mobile application on iOS. We are an independent developer committed to your privacy. We have written this policy to be as clear and complete as possible.

By downloading or using WordHaven you acknowledge you have read this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the app.

2. Information We Collect — None

WordHaven itself collects zero personal information. Specifically, we do NOT collect, process, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, username, or any identity information
  • Your phone number, address, or payment information
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Your health, financial, or biometric data
  • Any behavioural analytics or usage telemetry
  • Crash logs or diagnostics sent to our servers (we have no servers)

We do not require an account, login, or registration of any kind. The app is fully functional without any network connectivity.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To save your game progress between sessions, the app stores data exclusively on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

What is stored and why:

Storage Key What it contains Purpose
wordcraft_save_v1 Coin balance, current level, solved words, puzzle progress, tutorial status Resume your game where you left off
wordcraft_discoveries_lifetime_v1 Lifetime total of words you have found Display your all-time discovery count
wordcraft_discoveries_daily_v1 Words found in the current 24-hour window Show your daily word count on the home screen
wordcraft_discoveries_daily_ts_v1 Timestamp of your last discovery session Reset daily counter after 24 hours of inactivity
wordcraft_daily_progress_v2 Which daily puzzles you have completed and when Prevent replaying already-completed daily puzzles
wordHavenSettings Music on/off, sound effects on/off, reduce lighting setting Respect your personal preferences and accessibility needs

How to delete this data: Uninstalling WordHaven from your device permanently removes all locally stored game data. You can also clear the app's data via iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → WordHaven → Delete App.

4. Advertising — Google AdMob

WordHaven is free to play and is supported by optional rewarded video advertisements served by Google AdMob, a service operated by Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA). You choose to watch an ad in exchange for in-game coins — ads are never forced.

What AdMob may collect:

Google AdMob, as an independent third party, may collect and process the following types of data to serve and measure ads:

  • Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) — only with your explicit consent via the ATT prompt (see Section 5)
  • IP address — used to approximate your country/region for ad targeting
  • Device information — device model, operating system version, screen resolution, app version
  • Ad interaction data — whether an ad was shown, viewed to completion, or clicked
  • Approximate location — derived from your IP address only (not GPS)

This data is collected and controlled by Google, not by WordHaven. We have no access to it. Google may use this data in accordance with its own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in advertising
  • How Google uses data from apps that use Google services

Opting out of personalised ads:

You may opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • On iOS 14.5+: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off WordHaven
  • System-wide: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → toggle off "Personalised Ads"
  • You will still see ads after opting out, but they will be non-personalised (contextual only)

5. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework (iOS 14.5+), WordHaven will ask for your permission before allowing AdMob to access your Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for personalised ad targeting.

  • If you allow tracking: AdMob may use your IDFA to show you more relevant ads
  • If you decline tracking: AdMob will serve non-personalised (contextual) ads only — no IDFA is accessed
  • The app works identically either way — your choice has no effect on gameplay or features

You can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

7. Network Communication

WordHaven does not connect to any server operated by us. There is no WordHaven backend, cloud save, or account system. All game data is stored locally on your device.

The only outbound network communication the app makes is:

  • Google AdMob — to fetch and display rewarded ads (when you choose to watch one)
  • Apple's ATT framework — to prompt for tracking consent on first launch

All game content (levels, puzzles, word lists, backgrounds, music) is bundled within the app and loaded locally — no internet connection is required to play.

8. Audio

WordHaven plays background music and sound effects to enhance the puzzle experience. All audio is:

  • Fully optional — you can disable music and sound effects independently in Settings
  • Generated locally on your device (Web Audio API) or loaded from bundled files
  • Never recorded, captured, or transmitted

The app does not use your microphone or access any audio input.

12. Your Rights — GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent laws:

  • Right of access — the right to know what data is held about you
  • Right to rectification — the right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — the right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction of processing — the right to limit how your data is used
  • Right to data portability — the right to receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests

Because WordHaven collects no personal data itself, most of these rights are satisfied by default. For any data collected by AdMob on Google's behalf, you can exercise your rights directly with Google at myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.

For any questions or requests, contact us at wordhaven.app@gmail.com.

13. Your Rights — CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know — the right to request disclosure of personal information collected about you
  • Right to deletion — the right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out — the right to opt out of the "sale" of your personal information

WordHaven does not sell personal information. We do not share personal data with third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. AdMob's use of your IDFA (with consent) may constitute "sharing" under the CCPA — you may opt out as described in Section 4.
