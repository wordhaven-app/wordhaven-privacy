Privacy Policy

WordHaven  ·  Effective date: March 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Short version: WordHaven does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your game data stays on your device. We have no servers that receive your data, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

1. Overview

WordHaven ("we", "us", or "our") is a word puzzle game developed for iOS. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes what information (if any) is collected when you use WordHaven, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

By using WordHaven, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

WordHaven does not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information
  • Your location or IP address
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history, usage patterns, or behavioural data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any biometric data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to us

We have no backend servers that receive data from the app. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, and no user profiles.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

WordHaven saves the following data only on your device, using the device's local storage (WebView localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party:

  • Game progress — your current level, completed levels, and discovered words
  • Coin balance — your in-game currency balance
  • Settings preferences — such as sound and music on/off
  • Tutorial completion status — whether you have completed the introductory tutorial
  • Daily puzzle history — which daily puzzles you have completed

This locally stored data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app. We cannot recover it for you.

4. No Third-Party SDKs or Services

WordHaven does not integrate any third-party SDKs, services, or frameworks that collect data. Specifically, we do not use:

  • Advertising networks of any kind
  • Analytics platforms (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
  • Crash reporting services (e.g. Crashlytics, Sentry)
  • Social login or social sharing SDKs
  • Any payment or in-app purchase services beyond Apple's own StoreKit (not currently used in v1.0)

5. No Advertising or Tracking

WordHaven v1.0 contains no advertisements. We do not display ads, we do not use advertising identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA), and we do not participate in any ad network. We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

6. Apple Platform Privacy

WordHaven is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain information as part of the standard App Store and iOS operating system functions, such as crash logs or usage metrics, subject to Apple's own Privacy Policy. We do not receive, access, or control this data. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

7. Children's Privacy

WordHaven does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because the app collects no personal data from any user, it is appropriate for all ages.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information through the app, please contact us at the address below and we will promptly address your concern.

8. Data Security

Since WordHaven does not transmit or store any personal data on external servers, the risk of a data breach involving your personal information is effectively eliminated at the application level. Your game data is stored only on your own device and is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

9. Data Retention and Deletion

All locally stored game data persists on your device until you delete the app. To remove all data associated with WordHaven, simply uninstall the app from your device. Because we hold no data on our servers, there is nothing further for us to delete on your behalf.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR in the European Union or CCPA in California). These rights typically include:

  • The right to know what personal data is collected about you
  • The right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal data

Because WordHaven collects no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or opt out of. If you have questions, you are welcome to contact us and we will respond promptly.

11. International Users

WordHaven is available globally. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfers to be concerned about. All game data remains on the device you use, in the country where you use it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we introduce new features in a future version of the app. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If a future version of the app introduces any form of data collection, we will update this policy to reflect that clearly before the update is released, and we will seek any required consents at that time.
