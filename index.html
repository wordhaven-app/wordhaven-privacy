Privacy Policy

WordHaven  ·  Effective date: March 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Plain English summary: WordHaven collects absolutely no personal information. Everything — your progress, coins, settings — is saved only on your own device and never transmitted anywhere. We have no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

1. About This Policy

WordHaven ("we", "us", or "our") is a word puzzle game for iOS developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information is (and is not) collected when you use the app, how data is stored, and your rights.

We have written this policy to meet the requirements of the Apple App Store, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws. By downloading or using WordHaven, you acknowledge this policy.

2. Information We Collect

WordHaven collects no personal information whatsoever. We do not collect, process, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, username, or any other identifier
  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) or any other device ID
  • Your IP address or precise or approximate location
  • Demographic information such as age, gender, or language
  • Contact information, photos, or files from your device
  • Financial or payment information of any kind
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data sent to any server
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to us
  • Any data from your microphone, camera, contacts, or calendar

We have no backend servers that receive data from the app. There is no registration, no sign-in, and no user account of any kind.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide a functioning game experience, WordHaven stores the following data locally on your device only, using the device's WebView local storage (equivalent to a private on-device database). This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party:

  • Game progress — your current campaign level, completed levels, and discovered words
  • Daily puzzle history — which daily puzzles you have completed and when
  • Coin balance — your in-game currency balance earned through gameplay
  • App settings — your sound and music preferences (on/off)
  • Tutorial status — whether you have completed the introductory tutorial

This data is stored using standard browser/WebView localStorage APIs. It is sandboxed to the app and cannot be read by other apps on your device. If you delete the app, all of this data is permanently erased. We have no ability to recover it for you.

4. Network Activity

WordHaven does not make any outbound network requests to external servers. The app's word lists, level data, puzzle content, and all other assets are bundled inside the app itself and loaded from the device's local storage. No data is transmitted over the internet during gameplay.

The only network activity associated with WordHaven is standard App Store functionality handled entirely by Apple (e.g. downloading the app, automatic updates), which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

5. No Third-Party SDKs, Analytics, or Advertising

WordHaven v1.0 contains no third-party SDKs of any kind beyond the core Capacitor framework used to run the app on iOS. Specifically, we do not use:

  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs of any kind
  • Analytics or behavioural tracking platforms (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Segment)
  • Crash reporting or monitoring services (e.g. Crashlytics, Sentry, Bugsnag)
  • Social login SDKs (e.g. Facebook, Google, Apple Sign-In)
  • Cloud storage or sync services
  • Any payment or in-app purchase SDK (no purchases are offered in v1.0)

The Capacitor framework is an open-source runtime that enables the app to run on iOS. It does not collect personal data. Its own privacy manifest (required by Apple) is included in the app bundle and declares no data collection.

6. No Advertising or Tracking

WordHaven v1.0 contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not use Apple's Advertising Identifier (IDFA), and the app does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission. We do not track you across apps or websites, and we do not participate in any advertising network or data broker relationship.

7. Apple Platform

WordHaven is distributed exclusively through the Apple App Store. Apple may independently collect certain information as part of standard iOS and App Store operations — such as download records, crash logs submitted through iOS Settings, or aggregate usage data — subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive, access, or control any such data.

When you use Background App Refresh, iOS audio session management, or other iOS system features, these are handled entirely by iOS and Apple. WordHaven does not receive any personal data from these interactions.

8. Children's Privacy

WordHaven does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction, such as 16 under GDPR). Because the app collects no personal data from any user of any age, it is appropriate for all ages and families.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of the app, please contact us. Since we hold no data about any user, there is nothing we would need to delete, but we are happy to address your concerns directly.

9. Data Security

Because WordHaven does not transmit or store personal data on any external server, the risk of a data breach involving your personal information is eliminated at the application level. Your locally stored game data is protected by your device's own security features, including your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID, and by Apple's app sandboxing.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored solely on your device for as long as you have the app installed. To permanently delete all data associated with WordHaven, simply uninstall the app from your device. Because we hold no data on our servers, there is nothing we need to delete on our end — uninstalling the app removes everything completely.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you reside, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including:

  • GDPR (EU/EEA/UK): right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your personal data; right to object to processing; right to withdraw consent
  • CCPA (California): right to know what personal data is collected, right to delete, right to opt out of sale, right to non-discrimination
  • Other jurisdictions: similar rights under local law

Because WordHaven collects no personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, erase, port, or opt out of. All of these rights are already satisfied by the nature of the app. If you have any questions or want written confirmation, please contact us and we will respond promptly.

12. International Users

WordHaven is available globally through the Apple App Store. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfers to disclose. Your game data stays on the device you use, in the country where you use it, under your sole control.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if future versions of the app introduce new features. Any material change that involves the collection of personal data will be clearly disclosed before that version is released, and any legally required consents will be sought at that time. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
