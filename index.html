Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Summary

WordHaven is a word puzzle game built to be calm, focused, and private. We have no interest in your personal data and we don't collect any. Your progress, coins, and settings stay entirely on your phone and never leave it.

The one thing worth knowing upfront is that we show optional rewarded ads through Google AdMob. You choose when to watch one to earn in-game coins. AdMob works independently of us and may collect some basic device information to serve those ads. Before any of that happens, your phone will show you a permission prompt and you can simply say no. The game works exactly the same either way.

That's really it. No accounts, no tracking, no data being sold. The sections below go into full detail for anyone who wants it.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how WordHaven ("we", "our", "the app", or "the developer") handles information when you use the WordHaven iOS application. We are an independent developer committed to protecting your privacy under applicable Canadian law and international privacy standards.

By downloading or using WordHaven you acknowledge that you have read this policy. If you do not agree with it, please uninstall the app.

This policy applies to the WordHaven iOS app distributed through the Apple App Store. It does not apply to any third-party websites or services linked from within the app, including Google's advertising services.

2. What We Collect (Short Answer: Nothing)

WordHaven itself collects zero personal information. We do not collect, process, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, username, or any identity information
  • Your phone number, mailing address, or payment details
  • Your precise or approximate GPS location
  • Your contacts, photos, camera access, or microphone input
  • Your health, financial, or biometric data
  • Any behavioural analytics or usage telemetry
  • Crash logs or diagnostics sent to our servers (we operate no servers at all)

The app does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind. It is fully playable without an internet connection.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To save your progress between sessions, the app stores some data directly on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your phone and is never sent to us or any third party.

What is stored and why:

What is stored Contents Purpose
Game save Your coin balance, current campaign level, solved words, puzzle progress, and whether you have completed the tutorial So you can pick up where you left off
Lifetime discoveries The total number of words you have found since installing the app Displayed as your all-time discovery count on the home screen
Daily discoveries Words found in the current 24-hour window, plus a timestamp of your last session Shows your daily word count and resets it after 24 hours of inactivity
Daily puzzle record Which daily puzzles you have completed and when Prevents replaying a puzzle you have already finished that day
Settings Music on/off, sound effects on/off, reduce lighting preference Respects your personal preferences and accessibility choices between sessions

How to delete your local data: Uninstalling WordHaven from your device permanently removes all locally stored game data. You can also do this via iOS Settings > General > iPhone Storage > WordHaven > Delete App.

4. Advertising and Google AdMob

WordHaven is free to play and is supported by optional rewarded video advertisements served by Google AdMob, a service operated by Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA). Ads are never forced on you — you actively choose to watch one in exchange for in-game coins.

What AdMob may collect:

Google AdMob is an independent third party and may collect the following data to deliver and measure ads. WordHaven has no access to any of this information:

  • Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) — collected only if you grant permission via the ATT prompt described in Section 5
  • IP address — used to approximate your country or region for ad targeting purposes
  • Device information including device model, iOS version, screen size, and app version
  • Ad interaction data such as whether an ad was shown, completed, or tapped
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address only (not from GPS or precise location services)

Google controls this data and handles it under its own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in advertising
  • How Google uses data from apps that use Google services

Opting out of personalised ads:

  • Per-app opt-out: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > turn off WordHaven
  • System-wide opt-out: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > turn off Personalised Ads
  • If you opt out you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalised and based only on context

5. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework (required on iOS 14.5 and later), WordHaven will show you a permission prompt the first time you launch the app. This prompt asks whether AdMob may access your Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) to show personalised ads.

  • If you allow tracking, AdMob may use your IDFA to show you ads that are more relevant to your interests
  • If you decline tracking, AdMob will show contextual ads only and your IDFA will not be accessed
  • Your choice has absolutely no effect on gameplay, features, or your ability to earn coins by watching ads

You can change this decision at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

7. Network Communication

WordHaven does not connect to any server we operate. There is no WordHaven backend, no cloud save, and no account system of any kind. All game data lives on your device.

The only outbound network activity the app generates is:

  • Connecting to Google AdMob's servers to fetch and play a rewarded ad (only when you choose to watch one)
  • Communicating with Apple's ATT framework to handle the tracking permission prompt on first launch

All game content including levels, daily puzzles, word lists, background images, and music is bundled inside the app itself. No internet connection is required to play.

8. Sound and Music

WordHaven includes background music and sound effects to enhance the experience. All audio in the app is:

  • Completely optional — music and sound effects can be turned off independently in Settings
  • Either loaded from files bundled within the app or generated locally on your device using the Web Audio API
  • Never recorded, captured, analysed, or transmitted anywhere

The app does not access your microphone and has no capability to capture audio input of any kind.

9. In-App Purchases

WordHaven does not currently offer any in-app purchases. There are no paid upgrades, coin packs, or subscriptions. Your only way to earn coins is through normal gameplay (completing levels and finding words) or by choosing to watch an optional rewarded ad.

If in-app purchases are introduced in a future update, this policy will be updated and you will be informed through the app update release notes.

11. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

WordHaven is a word puzzle game intended for general audiences. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided any personal information through this app, please reach out to us at wordhavencontact@gmail.com and we will address it promptly.

12. Rights for Canadian Users (PIPEDA)

As a developer based in Ontario, Canada, we are subject to Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). PIPEDA establishes ten fair information principles that govern how personal information is collected, used, and disclosed.

Because WordHaven collects no personal information from users, these principles are satisfied by design. Specifically:

  • Accountability: We are responsible for the personal information under our control. Since we collect none, our exposure is zero.
  • Identifying purposes: Any purpose for which information is collected will be documented here before collection begins.
  • Consent: The only data-related consent we seek is your optional permission for AdMob's tracking via the ATT prompt.
  • Limiting collection: We collect only what is necessary. At present, that is nothing from our side.
  • Safeguards: All game data remains on your device and is protected by iOS's built-in security features.
  • Openness: This policy publicly describes our practices in full.
  • Individual access: Since we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing to request access to from us.

If you have a question or complaint about our privacy practices under PIPEDA, you may contact us at wordhavencontact@gmail.com. You also have the right to escalate a complaint to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

15. Data Security

Because WordHaven stores all game data locally on your device and we operate no servers, a server-side breach affecting your information through WordHaven is simply not possible.

Your locally stored data is protected by iOS's built-in encryption, Secure Enclave, and any passcode or biometric lock you have set on your device. We recommend keeping both iOS and the WordHaven app updated to the latest version.

16. Data Retention

WordHaven retains no data on its end because we collect none. The data stored locally on your device persists until you delete the app or clear its storage manually (see Section 3 for how to do this).

Any data collected by AdMob is retained according to Google's own policies, which you can review at policies.google.com/privacy.

17. International Availability

WordHaven is available worldwide on the Apple App Store. Because we collect no personal data ourselves, we make no cross-border data transfers.
